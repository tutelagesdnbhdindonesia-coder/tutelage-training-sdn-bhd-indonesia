<!-- BANKSMAN -->
<section id="banksman">
<h2>Banksman Training (API-U Approved)</h2>
<p>
This course provides essential knowledge and practical understanding of banksman duties during lifting
operations. Participants will learn safe communication methods, hand signals, radio procedures,
hazard identification, and control measures based on real operational scenarios.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Duration: 2 Days</li>
<li>Delivery: Online (Live) & Classroom</li>
<li>Certification: API-U Approved (Training Completion)</li>
</ul>
</section>

<!-- RIGGING -->
<section id="rigging">
<h2>Rigging & Slinging Training (API-U Approved)</h2>
<p>
This course focuses on correct rigging techniques, sling selection, load calculation,
inspection requirements, and safe lifting practices. Training includes real case studies
from offshore, construction, and industrial lifting operations.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Duration: 2 Days</li>
<li>Delivery: Online (Live) & Classroom</li>
<li>Certification: API-U Approved (Training Completion)</li>
</ul>
</section>
